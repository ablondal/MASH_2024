Leet (or "1337") speaking is a form of symbolic writing in which letters are replaced with numbers. It is a playful way of sending messages on the internet.

The rules are as follows:

  • Replace A to 4
  • Replace E to 3
  • Replace I to 1
  • Replace O to 0
  • Replace S to 5
  • Replace T to 7
  • Replace Z to 2

Additionally, change all remaining letters to uppercase.

Input

A single line containing a string consisting of upper/lowercase letters, numbers and whitespaces. Total length of the string does not exceed 5000.

Output

Output a single line of string consisting of uppercase letters and numbers.

Sample Input 1

New years Mash programming contest

Sample Output 1

N3W Y34R5 M45H PR0GR4MM1NG C0N7357
